# MVP Scope  MIMO Laundry OS

## In MVP
- Multi-hub-ready architecture
- Zone-based operating model
- Customer web ordering and tracking
- Affiliate order capture and shop handoff workflow
- Driver mobile task workflow
- Hub intake, processing, QC, and dispatch workflow
- Admin management for users, hubs, affiliates, drivers, zones, pricing, payouts, and basic accounting
- Versioned pricing with quote vs final outcome
- Immutable order event timeline
- RBAC, resource scoping, and audit logging
- Basic receipts, commission ledger, and operational reporting
- Bilingual UI support (English and Swahili)

## Explicitly Not in MVP
- Auto-dispatch or route optimization
- Live GPS map tracking for customers
- Customer native mobile app
- Corporate accounts and subscription billing
- Item-level garment tracking beyond bag-level workflow
- Advanced accounting suite beyond operational bookkeeping basics
- AI chatbot or AI customer support
- Dynamic surge pricing
- Marketplace onboarding automation for affiliates
- Driver incentive engine
- Warehouse robotics or IoT machine integrations
- Complex SLA prediction engine
- Multi-currency support beyond TZS-first operation
- Deep analytics warehouse / BI platform
- Public API for third parties
- Offline-first sync engine
- Automated fraud scoring

## Scope Guardrail
Anything not listed in **In MVP** is out of scope unless added through an explicit future chapter or backlog decision.
