# Driver Portal Copy EN/SW  Mimo Phase 2

## 1) Driver Portal Copy Principles

- EN must be short, clear, action-first
- SW must be natural and operationally usable
- no robotic translation
- no finance jargon overload
- no long explanations during execution flows
- every line must reduce hesitation and speed up action
