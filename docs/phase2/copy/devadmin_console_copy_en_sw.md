# DevAdmin Console Copy EN/SW  Mimo Phase 2

## 1) DevAdmin Copy Principles

- EN must be short, calm, precise
- SW must be natural and operationally understandable
- no robotic translation
- no raw technical dump as primary wording
- no casual language for high-sensitivity actions
- every line must support safe diagnosis and controlled action

## 6) DevAdmin Copy Lock Statement

The DevAdmin copy baseline is locked to short, calm, precise operational language in English and natural, support-usable Swahili. The wording supports diagnosis before action, makes high-sensitivity steps feel deliberate, and keeps internal controls readable without turning the experience into a raw engineering surface.
