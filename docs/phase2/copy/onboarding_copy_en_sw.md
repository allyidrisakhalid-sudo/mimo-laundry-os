# Onboarding Copy EN/SW  Mimo Phase 2

## 1) Onboarding Copy Principles

- EN must be short, premium, calm, clear
- SW must be natural and operationally understandable
- no robotic translation
- no tutorial jargon
- no long motivational paragraphs
- every line must support self-starting behavior

## 6) Onboarding Copy Lock Statement

The onboarding and setup copy baseline is locked to short, premium, implementation-ready EN and SW text that helps users self-start with minimal friction. Copy must stay calm, direct, and role-correct, and must never expand into tutorial-heavy language or duplicate help-center content.
