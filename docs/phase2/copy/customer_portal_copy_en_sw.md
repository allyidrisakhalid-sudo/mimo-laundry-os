# Customer Portal Copy EN/SW  Mimo Phase 2

## 1) Customer Portal Copy Principles

- EN must be premium, short, clear
- SW must be natural and customer-friendly
- no robotic translation
- no operational jargon overload
- every line must support confidence, action, or clarity

## 7) Customer Portal Copy Lock Statement

The customer portal copy is locked as short, premium, calm, and action-oriented in both English and Swahili. The language supports daily use, reinforces trust, and keeps the customer moving without jargon, clutter, or robotic translation.
