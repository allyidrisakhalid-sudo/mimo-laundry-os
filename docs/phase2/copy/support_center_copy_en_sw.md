# Support Center Copy EN/SW  Mimo Phase 2

## 1) Support Copy Principles

- all support copy must stay short, calm, and specific
- customer-facing support text must reduce uncertainty without overpromising
- English must feel premium, respectful, and operationally clear
- Swahili must feel natural, warm, and customer-safe
- refund and credit language must remain financially accurate
- internal complexity must not leak into customer-facing copy
- all copy must support mobile readability
- bilingual parity is mandatory

## 6) Support Copy Lock Statement

The support copy system is locked to a short, respectful, bilingual model that keeps support visible inside order context, gives admin clear operational language for triage and resolution, and preserves financially accurate refund and credit wording. All messages are implementation-ready for calm customer trust, premium readability, and EN/SW parity.
