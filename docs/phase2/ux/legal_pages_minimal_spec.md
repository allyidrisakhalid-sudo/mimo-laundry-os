# Legal Pages Minimal Spec  Mimo Phase 2

## Route: /terms

### Purpose
- state the basic service terms in a clean, readable way

### Header structure
- page title
- one short intro line
- no marketing CTA block

### Content structure
- sectioned document format
- readable narrow content width
- clear headings
- calm tone
- no decorative visuals beyond minimal brand framing

Recommended section outline:
- About these terms
- Using the service
- Orders and handoff
- Pricing and payment basics
- Customer responsibilities
- Service changes
- Contact

### Tone rules
- plain language first
- legally respectful
- readable by ordinary users
- not hostile
- not overloaded with unnecessary complexity

### Footer behavior
- same public footer family
- legal pages remain part of the same site system

### Forbidden patterns
- giant legal walls with no spacing
- marketing banners inside policy pages
- duplicate FAQ blocks inside policies
- hidden or hard-to-find refund language

## Legal Page System Rules

Shared legal page rules:
- all three routes must feel like part of the same public brand family
- policy pages must prioritize readability over decoration
- document width must stay comfortably narrow on large screens
- headings and spacing must break up content into readable sections
- legal content must remain separate from the main help FAQ body
- footer navigation must preserve easy movement back to core public routes

## Legal Page Lock Statement

The Phase 2 legal surfaces are locked as minimal, readable, plain-language policy pages under /terms, /privacy, and /refund-policy. They must stay calm, discoverable, and non-promotional while remaining visually consistent with the Midnight Silk public site system.
